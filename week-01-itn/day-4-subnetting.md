# Subnetting Basics

## What is Subnetting?
Splitting one network into smaller networks

---

## CIDR Values
- /24 = 256 addresses
- /25 = 128 addresses
- /26 = 64 addresses

---

## Example
192.168.1.0/24 → split into:

- 192.168.1.0/26
- 192.168.1.64/26
- 192.168.1.128/26
- 192.168.1.192/26

---

## Why important
- Better network control
- Less broadcast traffic
